Hunters and the Hunted

“There’s always a bigger bounty.”

Premise

The crew starts as bounty hunters chasing marks across the Outer Rim. But before long, someone places a massive bounty on them. Now every rival hunter and guild agent is on their trail. To survive, they’ll need to uncover who’s behind it—and why.

Themes

  • Cat-and-mouse tension
  • Rivalries and betrayal
  • The price of reputation

Major Factions

  • Bounty Hunter Guild: Enforcing the Code.
  • Syndicates: Hiring hunters for dirty jobs.
  • ISB Agents: Manipulating events from the shadows.

Long-Term Arc

Unmask the one who wants them dead, and decide whether to take revenge—or make an even more dangerous deal.
