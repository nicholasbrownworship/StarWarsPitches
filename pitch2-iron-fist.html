The Iron Fist of the Empire

“Smugglers, rebels, criminals… all crushed beneath the boot.”

Premise

In a backwater sector, a ruthless Imperial Moff is tightening control. Hyperlanes are patrolled, tariffs skyrocket, and stormtroopers enforce Imperial law at blaster-point. The crew can profit by smuggling contraband to desperate colonies—or risk siding with local resistance fighters.

Themes

  • Oppression and tyranny
  • Rebellion and survival
  • Life under the Empire

Major Factions

  • Sector Moff & ISB: Imposing control with brutal efficiency.
  • Hutt-backed Syndicates: Profiting from Imperial restrictions.
  • Local Resistance Cells: Struggling against occupation.

Long-Term Arc

Will the crew profit from Imperial rule, or risk everything by fighting it?
