The Fractured Sector

“Three warlords, one lawless frontier. Plenty of work for scoundrels.”

Premise

In a sector on the edge of the galaxy, three powers fight for dominance: a corrupt Imperial Moff, a Hutt kajidic, and a ruthless Black Sun Vigo. The crew takes jobs in the middle of this struggle, balancing double-crosses, shifting alliances, and dangerous rivals.

Themes

  • Intrigue and betrayal
  • Mercenary morality
  • Faction power struggles

Major Factions

  • Imperial Garrison: Corrupt, but still powerful.
  • Gorakka Hutt Clan: Expanding influence.
  • Black Sun Enforcers: Deadly and ambitious.

Long-Term Arc

Will the crew pick a side, play kingmakers, or burn the whole system down?
