Rebels in the Making

“They just wanted a payday… now they’re fighting a war.”

Premise

What starts as a one-off smuggling job for a mysterious client turns out to be Rebel Intelligence. Soon, the crew is running supplies, extracting spies, and sabotaging Imperial outposts. Each mission makes them more valuable to the Rebellion—and more hunted by the Empire.

Themes

  • Small acts, big consequences
  • Moral choices
  • Life on the run

Major Factions

  • Rebel Cell: Scrappy and desperate for help.
  • Imperial Security Bureau: Always watching.
  • Local Governors: Eager to prove loyalty to the Empire.

Long-Term Arc

Do they stay freelancers, or rise as Rebel operatives in the Galactic Civil War?
