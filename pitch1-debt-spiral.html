The Debt Spiral

“You owe the Hutts. Now pay up… or run.”

Premise

The crew inherited a beat-up freighter… and a crushing debt to a powerful Hutt kajidic. Every job is a scramble for credits—smuggling spice, running blockades, or pulling risky heists—while bounty hunters and cartel enforcers close in.

Themes

  • Desperation and survival
  • Crime underworld double-dealing
  • Hard choices and risk vs reward

Major Factions

  • Hutt Cartel: Your creditors.
  • Rival Smugglers: Out to steal jobs and clients.
  • Imperial Customs: Choking the hyperlanes.

Long-Term Arc

Can the crew claw their way out of debt… or will they sink deeper into the cartel’s grip and become permanent tools of Hutt power?
